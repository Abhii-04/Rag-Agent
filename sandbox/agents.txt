1. HISTORIC MILESTONE: AGENTIC BOT TRAFFIC SURPASSES HUMAN WEB TRAFFIC
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- On June 3, 2026, Cloudflare CEO Matthew Prince announced that automated
  (agentic) requests now account for 57.5% of HTML web traffic vs. 42.5%
  from humans — the first time bots have surpassed humans in web traffic
  history, arriving ~18 months earlier than predicted.
- Microsoft reported that AI-driven sessions it monitored tripled in 2025,
  calling the shift "historic."
- Cloudflare shipped countermeasures: AI Crawl Control, pay-per-crawl via
  HTTP 402 codes, a Content Signals Policy, and partnerships with Visa &
  Mastercard for agentic commerce security.
- Implications: advertising models, website monetization, and bot-detection
  infrastructure are being forced to adapt rapidly.

  Sources: Cloudflare (via Forbes/MediaPost), Microsoft, Cloudflare blog


2. MAJOR M&A: SALESFORCE ACQUIRES FIN FOR $3.6B
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- On June 15, 2026, Salesforce announced it is acquiring Fin (formerly
  Intercom), an AI customer service agent platform, for ~$3.6 billion.
- Fin's AI agent resolves customer queries across live chat, WhatsApp,
  SMS, phone, Slack, and email.
- The acquisition bolsters Salesforce's existing Agentforce platform,
  helping enterprises build and deploy custom AI agents.
- Fin serves 30,000+ companies globally. Deal closes in Salesforce's
  fiscal Q4 2027.

  Sources: CNBC, TechCrunch, Reuters, Bloomberg


3. INFRASTRUCTURE SHIFT: QUALCOMM BETTING ON AGENTIC AI
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Qualcomm CEO Cristiano Amon stated "agents are going to be the new app,"
  predicting AI agents will fundamentally change how users interact with
  devices.
- Agentic AI demands ultra-low latency and consumes far more tokens than
  generative AI, projected to quadruple CPU core demand in data centers.
- Qualcomm's "DragonFly" platform targets efficiency-first inference for
  data centers (advanced memory + liquid cooling).
- Qualcomm positions itself for on-device agentic AI across smartphones,
  laptops, wearables, and AR glasses (Snapdragon XR partnership with
  Snap's Specs eyewear).

  Sources: Forbes, CNBC Tech Download podcast


4. FINTECH & COMMERCE EMBRACE AGENTIC AI
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Visa & OpenAI partnered on agentic commerce infrastructure, allowing
  AI agents to handle the full shopping lifecycle — from product selection
  to finalizing payment with a Visa card.
- Lloyds Banking Group deployed an agentic AI fraud-detection system
  built on its Envoy platform; prevented over £1bn ($1.3bn) in fraud
  in 2025 and invested £100m in fraud tech since 2023.
- Ant Group invested $5.17B in R&D (2025) and launched China's first
  Agentic Commerce Trust Protocol via Alipay, alongside AI Pay and
  AI Wallet products.
- Cloudflare announced partnerships with Visa and Mastercard for
  agentic commerce security and payment infrastructure.

  Sources: Practical Ecommerce, FinTech Magazine, Ant Group Sustainability
  Report, Futurism/AP


5. IDENTITY & SECURITY FOR THE AGENTIC ERA
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- NewCore emerged from stealth with $66M in funding on June 15, 2026,
  building a security-first identity platform "rebuilt from the ground
  up for humans and AI agents."
- The platform addresses the challenge of managing identity, access, and
  permissions when both human workers and autonomous AI agents operate
  within the same enterprise ecosystem.

  Source: StreetInsider / PRNewswire
