1. APPLE WWDC 2026 — Siri AI & iOS 27
---------------------------------------
Apple's WWDC 2026 was its biggest AI event yet. The headline is the long-awaited
"Siri AI" overhaul — a truly conversational, context-aware assistant that:
  - Remembers previous conversations and can maintain multi-turn dialogues
  - Taps into personal data (Mail, Messages, Photos) via a semantic index
  - Can handle multiple tasks in a single request
  - Arrives as a dedicated Siri app on all Apple platforms (iOS 27, macOS 27,
    visionOS 27, iPadOS 27, watchOS 27)
  - Will support third-party chatbots (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude) as an option
  - Public release is expected fall 2026; beta is already available
  - iOS 27 will support devices dating back to iPhone 11
